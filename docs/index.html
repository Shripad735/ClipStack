Capabilities

Everything you need,
nothing you don't.

Five focused features that keep the overlay small and the workflow fast.

Smart history

Automatically groups clips by type, deduplicates entries, and keeps a tidy timeline so you never lose track.

Fuzzy search

Find any clip with a partial query. Results update instantly as you type, ranked by recency and relevance.

Pin & protect

Pin critical clips so they survive history clears. All data lives on-device — nothing ever leaves your machine.

Snippets vault

Save reusable phrases, boilerplate templates, and commands. Paste them in one keystroke from anywhere.

Export history

Dump your full clipboard history to JSON or CSV with one click. Useful for archiving, audits, or migration.

How it works

Three keystrokes.
Full clipboard control.

ClipStack stays out of the way until you need it, then gets out of the way fast.

01

Summon the overlay

Press Alt V from anywhere on Windows. The compact overlay appears instantly on top of your current window.

02

Search or browse

Start typing to fuzzy-search your history, or scroll through grouped clips. Filter by pinned items or content type in one keypress.

03

Paste with intent

Hit ↵ to paste immediately, or ⌘C to copy to clipboard. The overlay closes and you're back in your workflow.
